Shaming

This app will take in a League of Legends player name and try to shame him based on his last week ranked performance.

To use it - input the username (summoner id) into the field. Alternatively - go to /shamer/get-summoner/{input_summoner_id} The engine will try to fetch your required summoner from the RiotAPI database. If it does find him, it will then calculate stats -

  • Alone? - average kill participation
  • Poor? - average gold advantage
  • Blind? - average vision advantage
  • Loser? - average win rate

If more than two of those are true or he's a loser (cause losers are always shamed!) - the summoner is shamed.

Have fun!
