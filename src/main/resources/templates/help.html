Help

This app will take in a League of Legends player name and try to shame him based on his last week ranked performance.

Go to /shamer/get-summoner/{input_summoner_id} The engine will try to fetch your required summoner from the RiotAPI database. If it does find him, it will then calculate stats -

  • Alone? - average kill participation
  • Poor? - average gold advantage
  • Blind? - average vision advantage
  • Loser? - average win rate

If more than two of those are negative - the summoner is shamed.

Have fun!

Fetching data

All data is fetched from Riot's Europe servers - EUNE, EUW.

Data is fetched from the last week, only from the ranked solo queue.

If some data is corrupt and can't be parsed - it will be omitted in the final score.
